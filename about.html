Design Decisions

The design decision behind the carousel was quite simple. We wanted to create something more that just a simple carousel.
Given our limited knowledge, we decided that the best way to pull this off was to create an illusion.
This was achieved by applying a parallax moving background of a forest and a single image of a bat that is used twice in the carousel.
To further improve this effect, we applied a custom transition time for the images and flipped the bat 180 degrees to match the direction of the forest.
